Hi, I'm Marissa Bertrand.

I am a freelance UX/UI researcher and front-end web developer. Recently graduating from the University of Minnesota UX/UI bootcamp program, I am always ready to sink my teeth into a project.

With a BS in Anthropology from Emory University, I specialize in ethnographic research. Figuring people out, what they like, want, and wish - is what I love to do.

There's no such thing as one size fits all - employing design for the user is my focus.

Based in Chicago, IL, I'm a budding designer with a passion to create.
